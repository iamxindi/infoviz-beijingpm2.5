Explore pattern and factors of PM 2.5 in Beijing 2010 - 2014

Since year 2010, pm 2.5 has become the most concerned environmental topic in Beijing.
This visualization uses data from Beijing PM2.5 Data Data Set (https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Beijing+PM2.5+Data).
The purpose of this visualization is to show the time pattern and influence of different factors to the value of pm 2.5 in Beijing.

PART I: Temperature and wind simulator

In this section, you can play with different options in temperature, wind speed and wind direction and see how it changes the pattern of pm2.5.

*How to use this visualization?*
The animation on the left is a looped animation showing all the pm2.5 instances that correspond to the setting at a random order.
The bar chart on the right compares the ratio of "good" hours and "bad" hours.

PART II: PM 2.5 Spiral Chart

In this section, you can see a color coded summary of pm2.5 for each month in 2010-2014. The statistic is calculated using hourly data.

*How to use this visualization?*
1. Click on one of the button to change the summary statistic option.
2. Hover on the chart to see detail information.


*How to interpret the colors?*
Green to yellowish: under 100
Yellow to orange: 100-200
Orange to red: 200-500
Darker red: 500+
